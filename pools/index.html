About Elite Aqua

Swimming Pool Services & Consulting Company

At Elite Aqua Swimming Pool, we specialize in creating exceptional swimming pool experiences backed by quality, innovation, and care. Since our inception, we've been committed to delivering premium pool services, equipment sales, and expert consulting for both residential and commercial clients. Our mission is to keep your pool crystal clear and well-maintained, with dependable water treatment solutions that prioritize health and safety. Proudly serving customers from Nagercoil and across a 400 km radius, Elite Aqua is a name you can trust for expertise and satisfaction.

  • RO Plant installation, maintenance & repair (Domestic & Commercial)
  • Iron Remover solutions
  • Inverter & Water Level Controller servicing
  • Swimming Pool Equipment sales & expert consulting
  • Water Softener systems
  • Servo Stabilizer installation & maintenance
